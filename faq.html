FAQs

  Will we be taught by the mode of live webinars?
  Yes, all sessions will be delivered live on video conferencing platforms, in interactive mode.
   What kind of sessions will be there ?
   There will be three kinds of sessions.
  • Hands-on tutorial driven session in which the conceptual background on the topic shall be explained followed by demonstration through python code.
  • Some sessions will be problem solving, where you would be given a problem statement, and the solution shall be explained in a step-by-step manner.
  • Expert talks (total around 4 - 6) shall be delivered by well established researchers from academia and industry who will explain their own projects
  Is there any assurance that we will be helped thoroughly till we are able to develop the project?
  Yes, each participating student would join with other participating students of the workshop in teams and each team shall have a mentor who will help the team in resolving issues during the 2-4 weeks of the project.
   How will the doubts be resolved?
  Any questions you have will be addressed by the speaker live in that session itself either during the lecture or towards the end of lecture, depending on the speaker.
  Will we be given framework and other details regarding the project or it has to be our own brainchild?
  During the workshop, many expert talks shall be organized by well established researchers from academia and industry who will explain their own projects, from which you can figure out some ideas and you can also ask them a few project ideas.
  Will the sessions be recorded ?
  Yes, all tutorial driven sessions and problem solving sessions will be recorded. All expert talks shall also be recorded unless the speaker declines for recording.
  What course material shall be shared ?
  All recordings, presentation slides and related python code.
  Can you tell us who will be the speakers ?
  All the speakers listed have been confirmed except the speakers marked * are yet to be confirmed.
  Do I need to have the Internet and install any software ?
   Yes, you should have the Internet connectivity and you should install www.anaconda.com/products/individual Python 3.x version on your desktop/laptop.
  Is it mandatory to do ?
  No
  Will the project work done in it be treated as an internship ?
  Yes
   Is it online or offline ?
  Online
